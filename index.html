GOT Q's?

Click "Start 7-Day Free Trial" on the Shadow plan — no credit card required. You get full Shadow access for 7 days. After that you can choose to subscribe or drop to Free. Nothing is charged automatically.
Free lets you log up to 20 trades with basic stats — enough to feel the power, not enough to go deep. Phantom removes the limits and gives you full analytics, equity curve, notebook, and up to 3 accounts. Shadow adds the three features serious traders want most: Trade Replay, YzenBot AI, and Session Heatmap — plus Custom Models, Custom Nav and Checklist builder so the journal is fully yours.
Custom Models let you define your own trade setups — for example "ICT FVG", "BOS Retest", "VWAP Fade". Once created, YzenNq tracks the stats of each model separately so you can see exactly which setups are actually profitable and which are costing you.
Shadow users can reorder and rename the sidebar navigation to match their workflow. The checklist builder lets you create your own pre-trade rules — score every setup before entry and set a minimum threshold. Below your score? The journal says no trade.
The Session Heatmap shows you exactly which times of day and which sessions (London, NY, Asian) you make and lose money — broken down by hour. It's exclusive to Shadow and it's one of those features that will genuinely change how you approach your trading day.
YzenBot is built into the journal and has full context of your trade history. Ask it things like "what's my worst setup", "why am I losing on Fridays", or "summarise my week" and it will analyse your actual data and give you a real answer.
